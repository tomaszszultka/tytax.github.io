We are TYTAX a company that has been designing, manufacturing, and selling TYTAX training machines worldwide for more than 30 years.

All these years, our goal remains the same: to create equipment with unparalleled durability, dedicated to strength training, fitness training, cardio training, and stretching exercises. Our product range is completely different from that of other companies. You won't find single-exercise training machines or classic training racks with us, because over the decades of our company's existence, we have been creating training devices on which you can now perform hundreds or even thousands of exercises. As a result, each TYTAX machine offers classic body-building exercises, fitness training, and hundreds of unique exercises used in professional sports.

See an overview of the exercises you can perform on one of the machines - the TYTAX T1-X model Although TYTAX produces multifunctional machines sold mainly for home gym equipment, they are built to offer almost all exercises relevant even in heavy-weight training. See an overview of the possibilities of heavy-weight training on TYTAX, even for the strongest men in the world!

Each of the TYTAX machines has also a huge number of exercises for women. When you're a woman and you work out even at a well-equipped public gym, you don't get as many workout options for the muscles that are important as each TYTAX machine provides. With every model, you will perform classic exercises for the abdominal muscles and exercises that women especially appreciate - exercises for the thighs and buttocks as well as the calves. See an overview of super exercises for women, on the smallest of the TYTAX machines - the TYTAX S7.

Cardio training is important even for professional bodybuilders. Gym cardio equipment from the world's top manufacturers comes at a high price. In addition, it requires significant amount of space for training. This used to be our customers' dilemma - whether to buy a TYTAX or a good but expensive cardio training device. Plus, often the customer's home gym lacked space for a TYTAX and an additional cardio training device. We have solved this dilemma by adding cardio exercises to the range of exercises on TYTAX. And not just one exercise, but several types of cardio exercises, also ones that are great for HIT training. See an overview of cardio exercises that can be performed on the TYTAX T1-X

We provide our ultimate devices mainly for Professional Home Gyms, but also to other places, where there is a need to perform a safe and varied workout inside a small space. For example, private training spaces in a public gym or personal training studios. When such a place is equipped with a TYTAX T1-X or TYTAX T3-X machine you can even do a full program of heavy weight.

While others focus on all sorts of workout apps, gadgets, and applications, we have an unwavering belief in the power of classic strength and fitness training, which is in constant and growing demand around the world. For this growing trend, we have been building multifunctional, indestructible, and ultimate choice TYTAX workout machines for decades. This is appreciated by our customers, especially by wealthy individuals accustomed to a higher level of luxury and exclusivity in their daily lives, who give up exercising in a public gym and build their own exclusive gyms, equipped with TYTAX training machines, where they can perform their workouts in a private setting. However, strength training, fitness or cardio on TYTAX, is not only a benefit for health and building physical strength, but a combination of body training and intellectual fun - thousands of training possibilities and the joy of training comparable to that feeling when playing with blocks as a child. When you train on TYTAX, you are not restricted to using your own imagination and exploring new exercises. Training on TYTAX can be both fun and relaxing. By focusing on building and executing your own exercise ideas, you can get away from your daily worries and focus on enjoyable and creative physical and mental activities. Of course, if you need support from time to time, you can use the TYTAX training app to help you plan and monitor your progress. This is an additional tool that complements your training experience on TYTAX.
